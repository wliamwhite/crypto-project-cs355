About this project:

This site was created by four students from the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire. We collaborated to conceive and develop this platform as part of our coursework in CS355. Rooted in the essence of industry standards, our primary objective was not only to construct a functional application but also to implement cutting-edge methodologies essential for software development. To achieve this, detailed attention was devoted to making comprehensive documentation, strategically designed to streamline the project's lifecycle and ensure long-term sustainability. Instructions:

Pulling a Coin:

To pull a coin from the API, go to the text box to the left of the send button, If you can't find it, it says "Enter ticker to follow in real-time" inside. Once you're ready to type, type in the ticker of the desire coin. There is also a drop down menu to select from popular cypto currenies. Once ready to retrieve, hit the "Send" button. After coin is succesfully retrieved, it will display on the screen.


Coin not showing up?

If the coin is not showing up, either you entered in an incorrect value in the search bar, or didn't select an option from the drop down menu. Re-check the submission, if you believe you are 100% that you are doing it correct, reach out to our team. We respond fast and will get the issue resolved quickly.
